Hello! The following is the format that you must use to be accepted into becoming a Bridger Land Helper

Requirements:
- You must be 15 or older to apply
- You must have a working mic
- You must have access to a Minecraft Account that YOU own
- You must have the ability to record your screen
- You must be able to speak fluent English
- You must be active on all platforms (Minecraft, Discord and the Forums)
- If you are denied you must wait 1 month before reapplying, if this term is broken it may affect your applications in future

If you meet all of these requirements you can apply for staff, To do so create a post with the following title format: Helper Application: Username

Before using this format:

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Minecraft username ~

Previous Minecraft usernames? Check on https://namemc.com/ ~

Discord Username (Must include the tag, Ex #0000) ~

Date of the last time you applied for staff? (If not applicable put N/A) ~

Timezone and Country? ~

Age, and Date of birth ~

Do you have access to Discord, and a working microphone? ~

Do you have the ability to record? ~

Where are you most active on our Network? (Server (Which gamemode?), Forums, Discord - Which do you prefer over the other?) ~

Why do you want to be a Helper on BridgerLand? ~

What do you believe that you can contribute to the Network? ~

Do you have any skill set or experience that you believe could help out the staff team as well as the network? (Than documentation, event making, building, coding, configuration, etc) ~

What helps to keep you most motivated within a staff team? ~

Why should we choose you over other applicants? ~

Have you had any previous experience in any type of moderation or leadership? If So, Explain ~

If you were in a situation where you weren't sure what to do, how would you handle it as a Helper? ~

Scenario: A player who of which, is your friend, and they decide to break a small rule which is still punishable, what would you do in this situation? ~

Do you have any hobbies or activities outside of Minecraft? ~

How active can you be on the server? (Estimate Schedule on a Weekly Basis) ~

Are there any possibilities that your schedule is about to change (vacations, school/work-related, etc)? ~

Do you fully understand the concept of our Network and all it's gamemodes (MLGrush, Bedwars, Bridge Duos, etc.)? If so, could you be able to provide players with useful information when required? (As well as typical Minecraft knowledge)? ~

Have you had any previous punishments on our Network? If so, please explain. ~

Anything else you would like us to know. If so, Say it here! ~


------------------------------------------------------------------------------


If we like your application then we will look to contact you in regards to your application ASAP, however, in submitting your application you accept that if you continuously message admins to respond to your application it will be denied. You will also be expected to respond promptly if your application is accepted.
